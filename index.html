6th Africa Agri Expo.Adding Green to your Life.

Kenya is set to host the 6th edition of the Africa Agri Expo in February 2023, aimed at bringing the entire agricultural value chain under one roof.The two-day event is expected to open up a vibrant platform for agribusiness experts, top global companies and key policymakers to explore the commercial opportunities in the continent through effective meetings and networking sessions.

2023.02.6(MON) ~ 6(SUN)

@ KICC, Harambee Ave, Nairobi

Main Programme

Launch

Welcoming of guests and introduction to attending partners and organizations

Forum

Discussion on the latest irrigation and Agri-Technology, Agro chemicals, Inputs & Solutions

Exhibition

Network with various Agricultural Platforms and have one on one interactions.

Workshop

Showcase the inventions and all upcoming agritechnology

Closing

Award ceremony for competing participants, giveaways by organizers.
